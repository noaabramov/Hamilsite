About Hamilton

"Hamilton: An American Musical" is a renowned Broadway musical created by Lin-Manuel Miranda. The musical brilliantly fuses hip-hop, R&B, and traditional show tunes to tell the story of Alexander Hamilton, a founding father of the United States. The musical has garnered widespread acclaim for its innovative approach to storytelling and its diverse cast.

Set against the backdrop of the American Revolution and the early days of the United States, "Hamilton" explores themes of ambition, legacy, love, and the complexities of history. It features a diverse cast that reimagines the historical figures who shaped the nation's history, making the story both relatable and relevant to modern audiences.
